About Me

I am 25 years old and I am currently doing a Master's degree in Interaction Design at Aalborg Univeristy AAU.
I am curious about technology, open to new experiences, and I enjoy being challenged and developing new skills.

For the past 3 years I have worked as a gymnastics instructor at DGI-Huset Nordkraft where I teach kids to do flips. Beyond that I have worked as an assistant teacher at AAU for the course "Imperative Programming in C".

As I am nearing the end of my education I am looking for a job as an Interaction Designer / UI- or UX Designer. I'm available as a student worker as soon as possible or as a full-time employee from July 2023 which is when I finish my education.
